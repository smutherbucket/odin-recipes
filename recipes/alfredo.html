Making this Alfredo recipe will be quick and easy, feel free to add any protien you like to this dish.

Ingredients

    2 sticks of butter 1 yellow onion 1 garlic clove 12 oz of heavy whipping cream 16 oz of cream cheese 3 servings of fettuccine

Steps

  1. Melt the butter in a medium sauce pan
  2. Add in 1 whole chopped yellow onion
  3. Once the onion is cooked, add in the heavy whipping cream
  4. Reduce to a simmer for 30 minutes
  5. Last add your cream cheese
