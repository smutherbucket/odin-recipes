Etoufeee

This Etouffee recipe will have you make a roux out of butter and onions, an reduce it down with frozen crawfish tails.

Ingredients

  • 2 sticks of butter
  • yellow onion
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 1 green bell pepper
  • 2lbs of frozen crawfish tails

Steps

  1. Melt the butter in a medium sauce pan
  2. Add in 1 whole chopped yellow onion, and bell pepper
  3. Once the onion, and bell pepper is cooked, add in the frozen crawfish tails
  4. Reduce to a simmer for 30 minutes
  5. Serve over rice
